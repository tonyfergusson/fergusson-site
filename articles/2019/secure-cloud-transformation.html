Cloud Security

Secure Cloud Transformation — The Book

10 Feb 2019

Honoured to be one of the contributors to Secure Cloud Transformation — a real-world narrative for building the future in a cloud-first world.

The book tells the stories of how forward-thinking enterprises achieve scalable growth with direct, secure connectivity. The cloud has transformed how we work: connecting to resources from any device, from anywhere, with access that is fast, direct, and reliable. But that attractive productivity ideal challenges IT leaders who must maintain security and compliance while enabling that freedom.

Secure Cloud Transformation covers the architecture decisions, the cultural shifts, and the technology choices that separate enterprises that successfully make the transition from those that struggle. It is written by practitioners — people who have actually done this, not just advised on it.

My contribution draws on the MAN Energy Solutions Zero Trust transformation — one of the largest and most comprehensive ZTNA implementations in Europe at the time.
