Cloud Security

theCUBE Interview with Peter Burris

05 Aug 2019

I sat down with Peter Burris at theCUBE to discuss how MAN Energy Solutions approached its Zero Trust transformation — what drove the decision, how the architecture was designed, and what the outcomes looked like in practice.

The conversation covered the shift from perimeter-based security to identity-centric access, the role of Zscaler Private Access in making private applications invisible to the internet, and the broader lessons for enterprises embarking on cloud security transformation.
