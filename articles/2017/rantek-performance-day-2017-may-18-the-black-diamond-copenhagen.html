RANTEK Performance Day 2017 — The Black Diamond, Copenhagen

18 May 2017

Keynote speaker at RANTEK Performance Day 2017, held at The Black Diamond — the Royal Danish Library on the Copenhagen waterfront — on 18 May 2017.

Digital Transformation is not optional. For enterprises that understand this, the question is not whether to transform, but how to do it securely and at speed. My keynote addressed how security architecture must evolve in parallel with digital transformation — not as a constraint on it, but as an enabler.

Tony Fergusson is an infrastructure architect with over 25 years of experience in networking, security, and IT leadership. He was among the earliest practitioners to implement Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA) in production, leading MAN Energy Solutions’ award-winning transformation to Zero Trust architecture.
