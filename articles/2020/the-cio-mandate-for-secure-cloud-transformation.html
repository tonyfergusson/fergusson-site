The CIO Mandate for Secure Cloud Transformation

14 Apr 2020

A webinar with Nathan Howe from Zscaler, addressing the urgent shift to remote-first, cloud-native, Zero Trust architecture that the pandemic was accelerating across every enterprise.

The new way of work is remote, mobile-first, and cloud-based. Employees expect instant, secure access to applications, information, and resources — from anywhere, on any device. Meanwhile, threats proliferate, VPNs lag, hardware costs rise, and legacy security architectures are crumbling under the load.

Hub-and-spoke networks with castle-and-moat security are no longer tenable. Technology leaders that cling to legacy architectures put their enterprises at risk — not just from attackers, but from their own inability to scale and adapt.

The session covered the architecture principles for making the transition: eliminating VPN, adopting identity-centric access, moving security to the cloud edge, and building a posture that enables the business rather than constraining it.
