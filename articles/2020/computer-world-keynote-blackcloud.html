Computer World Keynote: BlackCloud

22 Jan 2020

Keynote speaker at the Computer World Security Conference in Denmark — covering both the Aarhus and Copenhagen dates in January 2020. The talk explored how the BlackCloud concept can protect the enterprise by making infrastructure invisible to attackers.

Aarhus — 28 January

Radisson Blu Scandinavia Hotel
Margrethepladsen 1, 8000 Århus C

Copenhagen — 30 January

Clarion Hotel Copenhagen Airport

The session addressed how enterprises can move beyond perimeter thinking — embracing a model where the network itself is dark, applications are invisible until authorised access is granted, and lateral movement becomes structurally impossible.
